Welcome to GoodVotes!

You are interested in sophisticated committee elections? This site is exactly what you are searching for!

With this tool you can easily create a committee election, and let your voters vote. Our goal is to allow the voters to express what they actually want to express. More precisely, we enable the voters to express not only that they like a candidate, but also preferences like the following examples in a very convenient way:

Substitution

I like potatoes and pasta on the buffet. But in case both were on the buffet, I would anyway only take either potatoes or pasta, not both. So having both is just as good for me as having only one of them.

Dependency

I really like the white curtains paired with the red ones. I would be in favor of buying the two, but only if we buy both together, because they don't look very pretty individually.

Incompatibility

Anna and Ben are both great engineers. One of them should sit on the expert committee. However, the two don't get along very well, and I think committee work would be disrupted if both were on the committee.
